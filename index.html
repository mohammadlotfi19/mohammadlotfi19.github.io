Hi! My name is Mohammad Lotfi, and i got my MSc under the supervision of Prof. Asghar Molaei Dehkordi in the Department of Chemical Engineering at the Sharif University of Technology (SUT). I got my BCs. in chemical engineering from the University of Isfahan, working primarily with Dr. Mohammad Hasan Khademi. My Masters research focused on modelling an electrochemical processes for carbon capture applications. I Worked on a new electrochemical method to capture CO2. This amine-based electrochemical system can selectively capture CO2 from industrial flue gas over successive efficient cycles.
